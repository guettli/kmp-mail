# IMAP LIST — enumerate mailboxes
# Source: RFC 3501 §6.3.8 example (adapted)

C: A001 LIST "" "*"
S: * LIST (\HasNoChildren) "/" "INBOX"
S: * LIST (\HasNoChildren) "/" "Sent"
S: * LIST (\Noselect \HasChildren) "/" "[Gmail]"
S: A001 OK LIST completed
